🏏 Sabaragamuwa University Cricket Team

The Sabaragamuwa University Cricket Team is one of the most energetic and talented university sports teams in Sri Lanka. The team represents the university in inter-university tournaments and other major competitions.

It is known for its strong teamwork, dedication, and sportsmanship. Players train regularly to improve their batting, bowling, and fielding skills. Over the years, the team has shown great progress and continues to bring pride to the university through their performance and discipline on and off the field.

🏆 Achievements

The SUSL Cricket Team has achieved remarkable success in inter-university tournaments, winning several championships and producing talented players who represent at national level.

👨‍🏫 Coaches/Captains

Our team is guided by experienced and dedicated coaches who focus on skill development, fitness, and teamwork. Their leadership and expertise help the players achieve excellence both on and off the field.
